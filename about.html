G Gyi's Story

G Gyi - Pet Shop was starting from since 2019 until now. I started this business because I love the pets really much and wanna share them who like me.
All of the pets we sell from our shop are bred in Myanmar but theirs parents are imported bloodlines.
Why we sell local bred pets?
Because they are match to our local seasons. Local bred pets are more cheaper than imported. Our target is for who love the pets to buy quality with fair prices.
